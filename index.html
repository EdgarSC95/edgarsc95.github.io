Problem statement: Students Finding Parking

Students have trouble finding unoccupied and don't know where to park at certain times of the day without getting intro trouble. An app to help display the best times to park and which parking spots are most convenient would be extremely helpful to those that have difficulties.

Feature-related Notes: Monitor traffic patterns: The app could track and analyze traffic flow to predict parking availability. User inputted data: Allow users to contribute data about parking availability. Cost of various parking spaces: Display pricing for different parking areas. Shows closest parking lot based on location: Provide directions to the nearest parking spot. Availability of parking lots: Show real-time availability of parking spaces. Repurpose Google Maps: Integrate with Google Maps for location services. Live tracking: Track the user's location to provide parking guidance. User Interaction and Security: User validation: Implement measures to verify the identity of users. Account security: Ensure user accounts are secure and private. Responsive UI: Design a user interface that is easy to use on various devices. Possible that patterns shown aren't consistent: Acknowledge potential inconsistencies in traffic pattern predictions. Account hierarchy system: Create different levels of access or privileges within the app. Operational and Data Integrity: Event tracking: Keep a record of parking-related events, like peak times or special events affecting parking. Version control for updates: Manage updates to the app efficiently. Inaccuracies in user input: Address and correct any wrong data provided by users. Abusive misinformation: Implement measures to prevent and manage the intentional provision of false data. Supplementary Notes: Needs to be scalable/maintainable: The app should be designed to accommodate growth and be easy to maintain. Show if reservations/parking pass is required: Indicate if a parking spot requires prior booking or a permit. Website/mobile application: The service should be accessible through both web and mobile platforms. Email/newsletters as advertisement: Use emails or newsletters to promote the app.
